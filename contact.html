About Star Alliance

The Star Alliance network was founded in 1997 as the first truly global airline alliance with a route network extending all around the world, an award programme and seamless services for international travellers. Its leading position in the aviation industry has been recognised by numerous awards, including the Air Transport World Market Leadership Award and the award for the Best Airline Alliance from Business Traveller Magazine and Skytrax. The Star Alliance Network currently offers a daily total of more than 19,000 flights to over 1,250 airports in 195 countries worldwide. Additional connecting flights are offered by the Star Alliance connecting partner Juneyao Airlines.
